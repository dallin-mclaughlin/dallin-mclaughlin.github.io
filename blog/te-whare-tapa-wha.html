Te Whare Tapa Wha

10 April 2023

Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing)

Every morning I will run around the neighbourhood. This will get the blood flowing and will help me pay more attention to the content presented in bootcamp.

Taha Whanau (Family Wellbeing)

Calling my family every Sunday in the evening.

Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing)

Taking time to reflect about what happened during the day. Type a journal on my phone before I go to bed.

Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing)

Praying in the morning once I get out of bed.
