EQ vs IQ

EQ is Emotional Intelligence. It explains a person's ability to perceive, control, evaluate and control emotions. IQ is Intelligence Quotient. It quantifies a person's overall mental ability

Importance of Emotional Intelligence

In the past people have argued that IQ was the major determinant for success in life, but now many people are saying that emotional intelligence is another major determinant for success. People are trying to compete for your attention in the market place of ideas and consumer products so understanding human psychology and how to persuade and how you can be persuaded are good skills in life that will lead to success.

Mindfulness

Mindfulness is becoming fully aware of the present. To not get so caught up in the past or future. Your body sensations and actions are focal points for learning how to be more mindfull.
